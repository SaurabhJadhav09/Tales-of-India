Description

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, is a picturesque blend of tradition and modernity. Known for its lush greenery, stunning beaches, and rich cultural heritage, Thiruvananthapuram offers visitors a glimpse into the vibrant lifestyle of Kerala. The city is steeped in history, with its origins dating back to ancient times. It was once a major center of trade and culture, attracting merchants and travelers from far and wide. Today, Thiruvananthapuram is a bustling metropolis that retains its old-world charm, with historic monuments such as the Padmanabhaswamy Temple, a magnificent example of Dravidian architecture, and the Kanakakunnu Palace, a beautiful palace that now serves as a venue for cultural events.
