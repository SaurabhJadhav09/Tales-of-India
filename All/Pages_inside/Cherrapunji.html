Description

Shillong, the capital city of Meghalaya, is a picturesque hill station nestled in the northeastern part of India. Known for its stunning natural beauty, pleasant climate, and rich cultural heritage, Shillong is often referred to as the "Scotland of the East." The city is set amidst rolling hills, lush forests, and cascading waterfalls, offering breathtaking views at every turn. One of the most famous attractions in Shillong is the Ward's Lake, a picturesque lake surrounded by gardens and a wooden bridge, perfect for a leisurely stroll. Shillong is also known for its vibrant music scene, with a thriving local music industry and a number of music festivals held throughout the year. The city is often considered the rock music capital of India and has produced several renowned musicians and bands.
