Description

Nangal, a city located in the Rupnagar district of Punjab, India, is renowned for its picturesque landscapes and vibrant culture. Nestled along the banks of the Sutlej River, Nangal is surrounded by lush greenery and scenic hills, making it a tranquil retreat for nature lovers. The city is known for its well-planned layout, clean streets, and a serene environment that offers a peaceful respite from the hustle and bustle of urban life. Nangal is also home to the Bhakra Dam, one of the highest gravity dams in the world, which stands as a testament to human engineering and a source of pride for the locals. The dam not only provides water for irrigation and hydroelectric power but also offers a stunning backdrop against the clear blue skies.
