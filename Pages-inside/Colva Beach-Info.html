Description

Entitled the “Queen of Beaches,” Calangute Beach, Goa is by far one of the most popular and mesmerizing beaches in the city. It is the largest beach and is located in North Goa. The beach is mainly famous for its vibrant atmosphere that is indeed unmatchable. Surrounded by beautiful palm and coconut trees, this beach is spread over an area of four miles along the Arabian Sea. Calangute is an ideal place for seafood lovers. You can find stalls in the town as well as on the beach serving seafood. You can also find several multi-cuisine restaurants all over Calangute. Veg food is also served in most of the restaurants. The best and most popular time to visit Calangute is between the months of November to March.
