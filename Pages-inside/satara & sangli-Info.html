Description

Satara is a district located in the state of Maharashtra, India. It is situated in the western region of Maharashtra, bordered by Pune, Solapur, Sangli, and Ratnagiri districts. The district is known for its hilly terrain, rivers, and scenic landscapes. Sangli is a district located in the southern part of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its fertile agricultural land, with the Krishna River flowing through the region.

Satara has a rich historical heritage and was the capital of the Maratha Empire under the rule of Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj, a prominent Maratha king. The district has several historical sites, forts, and temples that reflect its cultural and historical significance.Both Satara and Sangli districts offer a unique blend of natural beauty, historical significance, and cultural heritage, making them attractive destinations for travelers interested in exploring Maharashtra's diverse landscapes and rich heritage.
