Lonavala & Khandala Where Nature Whispers and Hills Sing

  1. Home
  2. Lonavala & Khandala

Description

Lonavla and Khandala are popular hill stations in the state of Maharashtra, located in the Sahyadri range. These neighboring towns are known for their scenic beauty, lush greenery, waterfalls, and pleasant climate.

Both Lonavla and Khandala are also renowned for their local chikki (a sweet snack made of jaggery and nuts), making it a delightful treat for tourists. Whether you're interested in trekking, enjoying scenic viewpoints, or simply relaxing amidst nature, Lonavla and Khandala have something to offer for every type of traveler.
