Description

Pune is located in the western state of Maharashtra, India. It is nestled amidst the Sahyadri mountain range, providing picturesque surroundings and a pleasant climate. Pune has a rich historical heritage and was the seat of the Peshwas, the prime ministers of the Maratha Empire.

Apart from historical sites and cultural landmarks, Pune boasts attractions like museums, parks, art galleries, and entertainment complexes, providing a vibrant lifestyle for residents and visitors alike.Major attractions include the Aga Khan Palace, Shaniwar Wada (a historic fort), Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati, Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum, and the Osho International Meditation Resort.
