The beautiful white marble structure is a perfect amalgamation of Persian, Islamic, and Indian architecture.The Taj Mahal basks in its own glory and in varied shades as the sun ascends and descends down the horizon.The architecture of the Taj Mahal truly expands and depicts the early Mughal designs which is a combination of Indian, Persian and Islamic design tradition.The calligraphy done over the walls of Taj Mahal are mostly taken from the holy book- Quran Sharif. Apart from the walls of Taj Mahal, the verses are inscribed on the Tomb of Queen Mumtaz Mahal and Emperor Shah Jahan.
