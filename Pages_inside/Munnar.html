Description

Nestled in the Western Ghats mountain range in Kerala, Munnar is a picturesque hill station renowned for its lush tea gardens, misty hills, and breathtaking natural beauty. Situated at an elevation of about 1,600 meters above sea level, Munnar's cool climate and pristine surroundings make it a popular destination for nature lovers and honeymooners alike. Munnar's landscape is characterized by vast expanses of tea plantations, which are a sight to behold, especially when the leaves are freshly plucked and the plantations are carpeted in vibrant green hues. Visitors can take guided tours of these tea estates to learn about the tea-making process and sample some of the finest brews.
