Description

Coorg, officially known as Kodagu, is a picturesque hill station located in the southwestern part of Karnataka, India. Famous for its lush greenery, mist-covered hills, and coffee plantations, Coorg is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. The region is known for its stunning natural beauty, with verdant valleys, gushing rivers, and dense forests that are home to a rich variety of flora and fauna. The Abbey Falls and the Iruppu Falls are among the most popular attractions in Coorg, offering breathtaking views and a tranquil atmosphere. Coorg is also known for its coffee plantations, which produce some of the finest coffee in India. Visitors can take guided tours of the plantations, learn about the coffee-making process, and sample freshly brewed coffee.
