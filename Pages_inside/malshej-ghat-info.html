MALSHEJ GHAT Nature's tranquil masterpiece

  1. Home
  2. MALSHEJ GHAT

Description

Malshej Ghat is a mountain pass in India's Maharashtra state that is part of the Western Ghats Range. Some of the many species of birds that call this area home are quails, cuckoos and flamingos. Beautiful waterfalls and lush valleys await exploration in the nearby highlands. Visitors who like to partake in exciting pursuits and learn about the local wildlife flock to this area. If you are in search of a peaceful retreat in the lap of nature, Malshej Ghat will prove to be the perfect spot for you.Within the Western Ghats mountain range, the Indian state of Maharashtra is home to a mountain pass known as Malshej Ghat. This region is home to a vast array of avian life, including quails, flamingos, and cuckoos.
