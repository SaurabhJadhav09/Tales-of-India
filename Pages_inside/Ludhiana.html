Description

Ludhiana, often referred to as the "Manchester of India," is a vibrant city located in the state of Punjab. Known for its rich industrial heritage, Ludhiana is a major hub for textiles, bicycles, auto parts, and hosiery industries, contributing significantly to India's economy. Beyond its industrial prowess, Ludhiana boasts a lively culture and a deep-rooted history. The city is home to several historic landmarks, including the Lodhi Fort, Phillaur Fort, and the Maharaja Ranjit Singh War Museum, offering visitors a glimpse into its storied past. Ludhiana is also renowned for its culinary delights, offering a delectable array of Punjabi cuisine. From hearty dishes like sarson da saag and makki di roti to the mouth-watering street food, Ludhiana is a paradise for food enthusiasts.
