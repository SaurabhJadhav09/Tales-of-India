Description

Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant metropolis known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and bustling urban life. Formerly known as Madras, Chennai is a city that seamlessly blends tradition with modernity, offering visitors a unique and diverse experience. One of the defining features of Chennai is its long coastline along the Bay of Bengal, which is dotted with beautiful beaches such as Marina Beach, Elliot's Beach, and Covelong Beach. These beaches are popular among tourists and locals alike, offering a refreshing escape from the city's hustle and bustle. Chennai is also known for its rich cultural heritage, with a history that dates back over 400 years.
