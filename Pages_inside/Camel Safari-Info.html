Description

Camel safaris in Rajasthan offer an exciting way to explore cities and villages, with camelback rides through deserts and cities, featuring stunning sunrise and sunset views. Jaisalmer, the Golden City, offers a unique experience amid rolling sand dunes. Bikaner's safari includes charming settlements like Naukh, Baru, Tadana, Kanasar, and Mohangarh, with interactions with locals and folk tales. Jodhpur also offers camel safaris, especially in Osian with ancient temples. Udaipur and Mandawa provide shorter camel rides, including Udaipur's route from Fateh Sagar Lake to bustling markets and Mandawa's architectural gems.
