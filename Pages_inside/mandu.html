Description

Mandu, also known as Mandavgarh, is a mesmerizing city in the Malwa region of Madhya Pradesh, India. Steeped in history and surrounded by picturesque landscapes, Mandu is a treasure trove of architectural marvels, making it a popular destination for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts. The history of Mandu dates back over a thousand years, with the city being ruled by various dynasties, including the Paramaras, the Ghurids, and the Mughals. This rich history is evident in the city's numerous monuments and structures, many of which date back to the 15th and 16th centuries. One of the most iconic landmarks in Mandu is the Mandu Fort, also known as the "City of Joy."
