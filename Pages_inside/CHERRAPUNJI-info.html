Description

Located in the 'Abode of Clouds', Meghalaya, Cherrapunji is an attractive tourist destination known for its rich culture, scenic landscapes, lush greenery, mesmerizing waterfalls and remarkable living root bridges.Meghalaya's fame stems in large part from the city of Cherrapunji, or Sohra as it's known locally. Cherrapunji Meghalaya, the world's wettest place in the past, is fascinating. Cherrapunji tourism's rolling hills, numerous waterfalls, and breathtaking Cherrapunji view point will leave you with lasting memories. Cherrapunji is perched on a plateau, at a height of 4,690 ft above sea level.
