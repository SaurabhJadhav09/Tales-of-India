Description

Chandigarh is a city and a union territory in India that serves as the capital of both Punjab and Haryana states. It is known for its urban planning and architecture, with its design being the work of Swiss-French architect Le Corbusier. Chandigarh is renowned for its modernist buildings and grid-like street plan, which includes several gardens, lakes, and parks. It is also known for its high standard of living, cleanliness, and well-maintained infrastructure. Chandigarh is a major cultural and educational hub, with institutions like the Punjab University and several museums and art galleries.
